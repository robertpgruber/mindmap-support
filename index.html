Mind Map Pro - Support

Welcome to Mind Map Pro support! We're here to help you create amazing visual maps of your thoughts and ideas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create connections between nodes?
A: Tap one node to select it (it will turn orange), then tap another node to create a connection between them.
Q: How do I move nodes around?
A: Simply drag any node to reposition it on the infinite canvas.
Q: How do I pan around the mind map?
A: Drag on any empty space (not on nodes) to move around the infinite canvas.
Q: What's the difference between free and premium?
A: Free users get 100 connections. Premium subscribers get unlimited connections for $9.99/month.
Q: Do my mind maps sync between devices?
A: Yes! All your mind maps automatically sync via iCloud across all your Apple devices.
Q: How do I delete a node?
A: Go to the main notes list, swipe left on any note, and tap "Delete".
Q: Can I search my notes?
A: Yes! Use the search field on the main notes page to find any note by its text content.
Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone/iPad, find Mind Map Pro, and tap "Cancel Subscription".

Troubleshooting

My mind map isn't syncing between devices
Make sure you're signed into the same iCloud account on both devices and that iCloud is enabled for the app in Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud.
I can't add more connections
You've reached the 100 connection limit for free users. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited connections.
The app crashes or freezes
Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If problems persist, restart your device. Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store.
