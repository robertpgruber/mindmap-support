Mind Map Pro - Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 17, 2024

Quick Summary: Mind Map Pro stores your mind maps securely in your personal iCloud account. We don't read, access, or share your content. Your data belongs to you and stays with you.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Content

  • Mind Map Data: The notes, connections, and positions you create in your mind maps
  • User-Generated Text: Any text content you add to nodes
  • App Usage Data: How you organize and structure your mind maps

Technical Information

  • Device Identifiers: Anonymous identifiers needed for iCloud syncing
  • Subscription Status: Whether you have an active premium subscription
  • App Version: Which version of the app you're using

Information We DON'T Collect

  • We don't collect your name, email, or personal contact information
  • We don't track your location
  • We don't use analytics or tracking tools
  • We don't collect browsing history or data from other apps

2. How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • App Functionality: To save, sync, and display your mind maps across devices
  • iCloud Sync: To keep your data synchronized across your Apple devices
  • Premium Features: To provide unlimited connections for premium subscribers

What We Don't Do

  • We don't read or analyze your mind map content
  • We don't share your data with third parties
  • We don't use your data for advertising
  • We don't sell your information

3. Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data Lives

  • iCloud: All your mind maps are stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Local Device: Temporary copies may be cached on your device for performance
  • Apple's Servers: iCloud data is stored on Apple's secure servers, not ours

Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses Apple's encrypted iCloud infrastructure
  • Your data is protected by your Apple ID and device security
  • We follow Apple's security best practices for CloudKit integration
  • Only you can access your mind map data through your Apple ID

4. Third-Party Services

Apple Services

  • iCloud/CloudKit: For data storage and syncing (covered by Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • App Store/StoreKit: For subscription management (covered by Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Apple ID: For user authentication and data access

We don't integrate with any other third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Data Control

  • Access: You can view all your data directly in the app
  • Modify: You can edit or delete any mind map content at any time
  • Export: Your data is stored in standard formats in iCloud
  • Delete: You can delete individual nodes or entire mind maps

Account Deletion

  • Delete the app to stop data collection
  • Your iCloud data remains in your Apple account (under your control)
  • Cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID settings
  • Contact us if you need assistance with data removal

6. Children's Privacy

Mind Map Pro is suitable for users of all ages. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you're a parent and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us.

7. International Users

Mind Map Pro uses iCloud, which stores data in accordance with Apple's global data practices. Your data location depends on your Apple ID country/region settings and Apple's data residency policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We'll update the "Last updated" date at the top
  • Significant changes will be communicated through app updates
  • Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes

For users in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your data is:

  • Legitimate Interest: To provide app functionality and sync services
  • Contract Performance: To deliver premium subscription features
  • Consent: You provide consent by using the app and accepting this policy
